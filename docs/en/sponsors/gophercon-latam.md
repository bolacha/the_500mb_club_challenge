# GopherCon Latam

*The biggest Go event in Latin America.*

- Official website: <https://gopherconlatam.org/>

## Who we are

Since **2016** — when the very first edition was held as **GopherCon Brasil** at Hotel Canasvieiras Internacional in **Florianópolis, Brazil**, on November 4–5 — this event has been a gathering point for brilliant minds in the Go community. Over the years it has grown from a national conference into **GopherCon Latam**, today the largest reference for **Go in Latin America**, with a consolidated venue in Florianópolis (CentroSul).

Every edition brings together **1,000+ developers** for 3 days of **keynotes, technical talks, and workshops** delivered by figures such as **Bill Kennedy (Ardan Labs)**, engineers from **Google**, and tech leaders from the top Brazilian tech companies. The audience is overwhelmingly professional (≈99%), with a meaningful share in **decision-making roles** (≈17%).

## Recurring themes

- Concurrency and goroutines in Go
- Performance tuning and observability
- Microservices with gRPC and REST
- AI / LLM integration with Go
- Kubernetes and cloud-native platforms
- Best practices, testing, and architecture

## Sponsors and ecosystem

Historical sponsors include **Globo**, **Stone**, **Mercado Libre**, **JetBrains**, **Google**, **Ardan Labs**, and other companies with strong Go adoption. The conference features an **800 m² vendor expo**, dedicated networking spaces, and social events — making it one of the most relevant meeting points for the engineering community in the region.

## Why it matters

GopherCon Latam is one of the few venues in Latin America where real decisions about architecture, performance, and Go adoption happen between the people who write the code and the people who choose the stack. For a challenge like The 500MB Club — focused on efficiency and engineering discipline — it's the natural stage where the "is Go really efficient?" debate moves from Twitter into actual numbers and benchmarks.
